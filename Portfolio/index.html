About Me


Michael Le, he was once working a dead end job as an accountant, but he finally decided to make a change. Now he is getting into the exciting career of coding. He loves taking complex problems and turning them into elegant coding, making order from chaos.

When you can’t find him behind a desk you can look for him in the skies. No, he doesn’t have superpowers but he loves to travel. He loves to experience new cultures and broaden his understanding of other people. He hopes to use that experience to create something that will benefit people.
